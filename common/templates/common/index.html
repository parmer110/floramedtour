FloraMedTour

FloraMedTour is a full-service medical tourism agency in Iran, an experienced team to help you discover the best medical centers that provide the highest quality of care for your healthcare issues. We only have partnerships with the best service providers in Iran, such as high-quality hospitals and clinics. The agency provides you with all-inclusive care, allowing you to relax and enjoy your medical tourism trip. from the moment you arrive at the airport in Iran, and throughout your stay in Iran, we will always accompany you.
